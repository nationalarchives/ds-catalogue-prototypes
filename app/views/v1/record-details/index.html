What's it about?

This record is about 20th Century Paintings and drawings: commercial from the series Records of the Copyright Office, Stationers' Company. It is held at The National Archives, Kew.

Is it available online?

No. This record has not been digitised and cannot be downloaded.

However, you can buy a copy or order it in advance to be ready for you when you visit Kew.
